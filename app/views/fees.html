Our Fees

  • House Visit - R120.00 per visit

    This entails a single visit to your house in order to feed your pets, water your plants or give your home that lived in look. Extra will be charged should you live further than 10km away from the Lonehill / Fourways area at R4.10/km.

  • Dog Walk - R250.00 per pet

    This entails collecting your dog, going for a 45 minute walk and then retuning him / her back to your house.

  • Pet Taxi - R160.00 per pet

    This entails collecting your pets and taking them to the parlor or vets and returning them. This costs does not include the bill related to the appointment.
